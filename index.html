This website is dedicated to hosting what I consider to be useful links, images, reference documents, etc. The central theme of information here is primarily on vintage audio electronics and test equipment. Other topics of interest shared here may include but is not limited to: musical instruments (mainly guitar), music gear reviews, woodworking, welding, and other shop equipment. The information contained here can be found on the internet for free, but I get tired of trying to remember all of the various resources to search when I need something. So, I hope you will find this collection helpful and interesting.
